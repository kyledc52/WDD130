U.S.R.P.T.
(United States Race Pace Training)

20x 50s @1min on goal time (200 pace)
20x 25s @30secs on goal time (100 pace)
10x 100s @2min on goal time (500 pace)

If you miss your time, you sit out for one and go for the next one (50s and 100s), and for 25s, you sit out for 2.
After completing work out, how many did you make? Record it and try it again later to see if you can beat your time.
